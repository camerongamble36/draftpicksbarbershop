TJ Malveaux

From Morgan City Louisiana, currently residing in Baton Rouge Lousiana. TJ's style remains on the cutting edge, which has helped him build an unwavering & rapidly growing clientele base in the Baton Rouge area.

He can be considered the "Athletes Barber" for the majority of men's sports at Lousiana State University (LSU). He has 30+ active NFL clients on nearly 15 of the 32 NFL teams and has styled his clients at the 2017 & 2019 NFL Drafts, as well as the 2019 Pro Bowl.

He also had the opportunity to be the stylist for several celebrities throughout his career. Sharp lines & smooth fades have been a trademark of his talent, as he continues to become a styling phenom in Lousiana and other parts of the US.
